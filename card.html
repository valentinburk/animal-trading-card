Macaque

Macaques are principally frugivorous, although their diet also includes seeds, leaves, flowers, and tree bark.

  • Scientific name: Macaca
  • Height: 57 cm
  • Lifespan: Up to 20 years
  • Mass: Up to 14 kg

Aside from humans (genus Homo), the macaques are the most widespread primate genus, ranging from Japan to the Indian subcontinent, and in the case of the barbary macaque (Macaca sylvanus), to North Africa and Southern Europe. Twenty-three macaque species are currently recognized.
